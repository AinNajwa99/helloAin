Favourite Movies

1. Cahaya Cinta Pesantren.

Cerita ni tak adalah menarik sangat cuma pengajaran yang aku dapat dalam cerita ni buatkan cerita ni jadi favourite aku. Kasih sayang seorang ayah terhadap anaknya. Walau tak mampu sediakan yang mewah untuk anak, ayah tetap mahu berikan yang terbaik untuk anaknya. Tak semua perkara boleh diungkap dengan kata-kata. Kadang kasih sayang sejati adalah kasih sayang yang tak terungkap.

2. Fast And Furious Series.

Cerita ni aku suka mungkin sebab nampak cool. Hehe. Lumba kereta semua tu. Tapi aku paling suka bila tentang friendship diorang. No matter what, Don tried to take care of everyone he loves. He did what he wants, what he loves, but in the same time, he didn't harm anyone else unless he needs to. Hobbs and Shaw version is the movie that I like the most. The dynamic actions are just too cool. And when Hobbs reunited back with his family, I feel great. Reuniting with family members after so long are just way too happy for me. Overall, it is their intelligence and advance technology are what making me in awe.

3. Wreck-It-Ralph Series.

My English teacher asked my whole class to watch this movie when I'm in form three and since then, I'm in love with this movie. Ralph character are just too cool. And for Wreck-It-Ralph 2, Ralph trying to help his best friend kept me in awe. He knew it is dangerous to do so but he still did it for his best friend, which is hard to find these days.

4. Hotel Of Transylvania.

I don't know why I love this movie either. Again, it's my English teacher who showed me this movie and I instantly fell in love with it. Maybe because the protectiveness of the father for her daughter that I like. And his thinking when he fell in love with a human in Hotel Transylvania 3 that kept me laughing. It's adorable to fall in love again after hundred years of being alone.
